# DopaQuest
Welcome to DopaQuest! 🎮✨

Level up your life, one micro-win at a time.

DopaQuest isn't just another checklist—it's an RPG for your real life, built on the actual neuroscience of how the human brain builds habits. We believe that self-improvement shouldn't feel like a chore; it should feel like an epic adventure.

How to Play Your Life:

⚔️ Embark on Quests: Your daily routines are now Quests. Tap those chunky, satisfying buttons to complete them and earn XP and Gold.

❤️ Protect Your HP: We ditched the brutal "streak-to-zero" system. Miss a quest? You just take a little damage to your Health Bar. Keep going to heal back up—you never lose all your progress from one bad day!

💰 Visit the Loot Shop: Use your hard-earned Gold to buy rewards that actually matter to you. Treat yourself to a late-night noodle run, buy a "Quest Freeze," or unlock fresh, vibrant app themes.

Equip your habits, defeat procrastination, and become the main character of your own life.
